A lightweight, secure, and privacy-focused browser extension designed specifically for **Flyff Universe**. KoalaFlyff enables seamless multi-boxing by routing keystrokes intelligently between your browser tabs, allowing you to control multiple characters effortlessly.

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔄 **Cross-Tab Key Mapping:** Press a key on your Main tab and have it instantly execute on your Support tab.
- 🎯 **Advanced Configurations:** Map up to 10 unique key pairs. For example, press `C` on your Main character to cast `Heal` on your Support.
- ⚡ **Global Toggle Hotkey:** Instantly pause or resume the background service with a single customizable hotkey (Default: `Ö`).
- 📊 **Session Analytics:** Automatically track your playtime and session durations to monitor your progress.
- 🎮 **Optimized for WebGL:** Built specifically for modern browser games like Flyff Universe, ensuring zero input lag.
- 🛡️ **Privacy First:** We don't track you. KoalaFlyff requires minimal permissions, uses entirely private local storage, and communicates strictly between your local tabs.

## 🚀 Installation

1. Go to the [Releases](https://github.com/Shik3i/FlyffUniverseHelper/releases) page.
2. Download the latest `KoalaFlyff.zip` file.
3. Extract the contents of the `.zip` file to a folder on your computer.
4. Open your Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Brave, Edge) and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
5. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
6. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder where you extracted the extension.

## 🛠️ Usage Guide

1. Open **Flyff Universe** in two separate browser tabs and log into your characters.
2. Click on the **KoalaFlyff** extension icon to open the popup.
3. Select your **Main Character** (the tab you will be actively playing on) and your **Support Character** (the tab receiving the commands).
4. Configure your desired key mappings (e.g., Map `1` -> `2`).
5. Click **Start Service** or press your defined toggle hotkey (`Ö`) to activate the routing.

## ⚙️ Configuration & Debugging

- **Custom Hotkeys:** Easily change the global toggle key directly in the Settings panel.
- **Detailed Logging:** Enable debug mode in the settings to view real-time keystroke routing information, helping you perfect your setup.
